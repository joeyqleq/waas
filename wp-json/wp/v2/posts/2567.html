The old posts are always working for you as well and that&#8217;s where you start to pay for your investment.<\/p>\n<p>There is an old saying in the record business that the catalogs of songs and records keep the lights on. We want to make sure a client is committed to the long-term blogging strategy before getting involved. It&#8217;s best for both parties to go in with the right expectations.<\/p>\n<p>We&#8217;ve talked about it before here on the blog. A business blog is something that is a long-term strategy.
