Many make it seem like there are silver bullets and quick fixes for getting more traffic, leads and sales.<\/p>\n<p>There are some folks out there that feel blogging can be a silver bullet for online marketing. If you&#8217;ve read through this website and have thought that at any time I apologize. That is not the intent on the content on this site.<\/p>\n<p>The intent is to provide information for how business blogging works, how it can work for you and what the proper expectations are for your business blog.<\/p>\n<p>Blogging is not a silver bullet solution. It&#8217;s not quick tip. It&#8217;s not a secret online marketing trick.<\/p>\n<h3>Business Blogging For Traffic, Leads and Sales<\/h3>\n<p>There is a growing segment of the business world that is accepting business blogging as a real marketing business model. Companies like <a href=\"http:\/\/www.seomoz.org\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">SEOmoz<\/a>, <a href=\"http:\/\/www.kissmetrics.com\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">KISSmetrics<\/a>, <a href=\"http:\/\/www.crazyegg.com\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Crazy Egg<\/a> and others <a href=\"https:\/\/ghostblogwriters.com\/business-blogging-effectiveness\">are using blogging<\/a>, guides and other forms of content throughout the sales process to get new customers. For these companies, business blogging is a way to grow.<\/p>\n<p>You can read about these success stories in business publications. Marketers have been reading the stories so business blogging really isn&#8217;t a secret anymore.<\/p>\n<h3>Business Blogging Is Not A Quick Tip<\/h3>\n<p>There are some posts around &#8211; including a few of my own probably &#8211; that mention blogging as an online marketing tip. You&#8217;ve probably read a few lists out there that have blogging as an item for businesses to do if they want to do online marketing the right way.<\/p>\n<p>While it&#8217;s true that blogging is good for online marketing it really isn&#8217;t a quick tip. In fact, there is nothing quick about blogging. It&#8217;s a long-term strategy so it&#8217;s important to put the expectations in the right place from the beginning of your blogging efforts.<\/p>\n<p>That&#8217;s why you see so many dormant blogs out there. Their owners get excited about blogging. They write a few posts. Then they run out of ideas or they realize blogging is more work than they originally thought and on top of it all they realize that traffic doesn&#8217;t instantly appear on your site.<\/p>\n<p>The truth is it can take six months to get traction with your blog. That&#8217;s how long it took for <a href=\"https:\/\/ghostblogwriters.com\/sarah-lynn-design-traffic-increase\">Sarah Lynn Design<\/a>. Even that is pretty unusual. It can take more like 12-18 months to really start getting the traffic you want.<\/p>\n<p>The blog on GBW accounts for a <a href=\"https:\/\/ghostblogwriters.com\/blog-analytics\">53% increase in traffic<\/a> on the site, but it&#8217;s taken years of blogging daily in a really niche market to get to that point.<\/p>\n<h3>No Silver Bullets<\/h3>\n<p>There are no silver bullets with blogging. In fact, there are no silver bullets in online marketing, offline marketing or in business. Even things like software and apps aren&#8217;t really silver bullets for your business. There is nothing that will replace hard work combined with a long-term approach.<\/p>\n<p>That&#8217;s how you have to approach business blogging. It&#8217;s how we approach it with every client by using the same process:<\/p>\n<p><strong>1. Discuss the Company, Industry and Customer<\/strong><\/p>\n<p><strong>2. Create a Blogging Strategy<\/strong><\/p>\n<p><strong>3. Provide Titles and a Schedule<\/strong><\/p>\n<p><strong>4. Deliver Risk-Free First Post on a Trial Basis<\/strong><\/p>\n<p><strong>5. Ask for Feedback on First Post<\/strong><\/p>\n<p><strong>6. Follow Ongoing Post Schedule<\/strong><\/p>\n<p>It&#8217;s a long-term strategy that works for our business blog and the blogs of our clients.<\/p>\n<p>Stop looking for a silver bullet in online marketing.
