The posts are incredibly practical and useful for everyday things. Okay. That might be an exaggeration, but seriously. The guy shares the amount of money he has in his <a href=\"http:\/\/moz.com\/rand\/inflection-points-bravery-vs-foolishness\/\">checking account<\/a>.<\/p>\n<p>I think we all wish we were that open and honest with our lives, but for some reason we like to keep things personal. I&#8217;m the biggest offender. I read Rand&#8217;s posts about his life and business and it makes me want to follow suit.<\/p>\n<p>Oh, and his wife has an amazingly <a href=\"http:\/\/www.everywhereist.com\/\">funny blog<\/a> too. And they <a href=\"http:\/\/www.everywhereist.com\/lambeau-field-and-the-packers-hall-of-fame-green-bay-wisconsin\/\">love the Packers<\/a>.<\/p>\n<h3>The All That Matters Is Profit Blog That Bucks All The Best Practices And Gets Results: <a href=\"http:\/\/blog.minethatdata.com\/\">MineThatData<\/a><\/h3>\n<p>If you&#8217;re not following Kevin Hillstrom on his blog and on Twitter you&#8217;re missing out.<\/p>\n<p>Following him keeps me in check.<\/p>\n<p>All you see out there these days is information about getting traffic and measuring KPIs and other things.<\/p>\n<p>All that really matters in business is profit. It&#8217;s about finding profitable ways to acquire new customers. It&#8217;s a simple concept, but one we can forget.<\/p>\n<p>Kevin also has a great perspective on the <a href=\"http:\/\/blog.minethatdata.com\/2013\/02\/channel-by-age-interaction.html\">demographical differences<\/a> in the media people use. Make sure your target customer is using the Internet before you invest in things like social media and even email marketing.<\/p>\n<p>Kevin appears to have a strong consulting business going. It&#8217;s no wonder because he knows his stuff and has the data to back it up.<\/p>\n<h3>The Most Confident Blog That Doesn&#8217;t Have Time for Haters Award: <a href=\"http:\/\/socialtriggers.com\/\">Social Triggers<\/a><\/h3>\n<p>Derek Halpern doesn&#8217;t take guff from anyone. Seriously. He wrote an entire post about <a href=\"http:\/\/socialtriggers.com\/how-to-deal-with-haters\/\">how to handle haters<\/a>.<\/p>\n<p>It&#8217;s a good lesson for us all. We can&#8217;t control what people think of us. It doesn&#8217;t really matter. It only pays to focus on what we can control and Derek does that well.
