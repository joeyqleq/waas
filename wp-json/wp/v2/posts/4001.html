Today is a good reminder that you&#8217;re in charge of your own destiny for the coming year. You control your own success. People out there will always talk about gloom and doom. Don&#8217;t listen to them. There is more opportunity now than ever before in human history.<\/p>\n<p>You just need to take advantage of it.<\/p>\n<p>So give yourself a gift this year.<\/p>\n<p>These are my recommendations for gifts you can give yourself that will pay dividends in the New Year.<\/p>\n<h2>1. A Business Blog<\/h2>\n<p>I&#8217;m obviously biased when it comes to blogging, but I truly believe it&#8217;s the best thing a business can do for an online marketing strategy.<\/p>\n<p>Ads can be a great part of an online strategy, but put an ad and a blog post side by side. Your ad runs only as long as you keep paying. A blog post, once published, is online forever for people to find through search and social.<\/p>\n<p>With social media your content might be seen by a few people. Whatever you share quickly vanishes from your feed and the feeds of your followers. And you don&#8217;t even own the content you share on social media.<\/p>\n<p>When you publish your own content you can use other channels to promote it. You own the content. And you can build your own subscription channel with an email program.<\/p>\n<h2>2. Buffer<\/h2>\n<p>For the last few weeks I&#8217;ve been testing the <a href=\"http:\/\/bufferapp.com\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Buffer<\/a> app for social media.<\/p>\n<p>I first found out about Buffer by a story about <a href=\"http:\/\/entreproducer.com\/buffer-app\/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">their blogging strategy<\/a>. Side note: That&#8217;s another good case for having your own business blog.<\/p>\n<p>Since using Buffer my own follower stats have increased on Twitter. Traffic to the GBW has increased. It&#8217;s been easier to keep the social feeds updated and it&#8217;s really easy to use <a href=\"https:\/\/ghostblogwriters.com\/the-snippet-strategy\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">The Snippet Strategy<\/a> by scheduling tweets with Buffer.<\/p>\n<p>Give yourself Buffer for Christmas and you should enjoy more followers, engagement and traffic in 2014.<\/p>\n<h2>3. More Personal Time &amp; Sleep<\/h2>\n<p>Over the last year I&#8217;ve noticed that there is a stigma in the business world. It&#8217;s not even just a business stigma. It&#8217;s a professional stigma. People are worn out it seems. Even if we&#8217;re not working all the time we&#8217;re stressed and strained. Our minds are shot.<\/p>\n<p>It&#8217;s often touted by people that they only sleep five or six hours a night as if it&#8217;s something\u00a0<em>good<\/em>. It&#8217;s not good.<\/p>\n<p>Earlier this year I took a few opportunities to work into the night to do work. The result was a huge crash the next day or later in the week. I ended up losing more ground than I gained by working into the night.<\/p>\n<p>This year, focus on getting more sleep. Take more personal time for yourself and your family.<\/p>\n<p>It&#8217;s one of my goals for the New Year. I&#8217;ve already started it by making sure I get to bed on time so I wake up feeling fresh and excited about the new day.<\/p>\n<p><em>Once again, Merry Christmas!<\/em><\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"<p>It&#8217;s the time of year for giving and while I&#8217;m sure you&#8217;re thinking of others this holiday season, take some time to give yourself some gifts.
