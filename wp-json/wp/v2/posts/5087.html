They&#8217;re on every golf course, but they&#8217;re the size of a small Frisbee. What are the chances of hitting a sprinkler with a drive? Not very good.<\/p>\n<p>But on this day the golf gods decided it was time for me to hit a sprinkler.<\/p>\n<p>I couldn&#8217;t believe my luck. Maybe you lose a big prospective client because there was one thing they liked about the competition that you didn&#8217;t have at that moment.<\/p>\n<p>Maybe you planned some great marketing campaigns for the new year only to see Facebook and Twitter change their rules on how you can promote to your followers.<\/p>\n<p>There are a lot of things that can go wrong in life. It&#8217;s not fair, but in the end, that&#8217;s how life works.<\/p>\n<h2>Good Breaks Vs. Bad Breaks<\/h2>\n<p>In golf I&#8217;ve found there are times when I get some really bad breaks. I hit great shots only to see them end up in horrible locations. That&#8217;s the example above.<\/p>\n<p>But there are other times when I hit really bad shots (I&#8217;m talking really bad) and the ball ends up in a great location; sometimes even in the hole.<\/p>\n<p>In the past I&#8217;ve kind of focused on the bad breaks more. I would make a stink about the bad breaks as if someone was going to care.<\/p>\n<p>But when good breaks would happen I&#8217;d kind of brush it off. I would think that maybe I was owed that good break for some reason.<\/p>\n<p>But now what I&#8217;ve learned is that good and bad breaks are just part of golf.<\/p>\n<p>And they&#8217;re part of life as well.<\/p>\n<p>You can control only a few things in life and one of them is how you respond to the breaks you get.<\/p>\n<h2>Keeping The Right Attitude<\/h2>\n<p>These days when I&#8217;m on the course I try to keep a positive attitude as much as I can. I practice and when I go to the course I trust my practice. I try to be confident in every shot. When bad breaks happen I shake it off as part of the game.<\/p>\n<p>And I do the same when good breaks happen. I try to keep it on the level and assess how my game is in spite of the good and bad breaks I&#8217;m getting.<\/p>\n<p>I think in life you&#8217;re going to find that you&#8217;ll get an equal amount of good and bad breaks. It might not seem that way, but you have control over your perception of the breaks you get in life.<\/p>\n<p>If you focus on the positive breaks you&#8217;ll find that you&#8217;re a pretty lucky person and that you&#8217;re good at what you do. That can lead to self-encouragement and from there you can accomplish great things.<\/p>\n<p>And even if that doesn&#8217;t work out all the way at least you&#8217;ll be happier every day.<\/p>\n<p>It&#8217;s worked for me on the course and I&#8217;m trying to do it in life and in business.<\/p>\n<p>So far so good.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"<p>Do bad things seem to happen to you more than they do to others?
