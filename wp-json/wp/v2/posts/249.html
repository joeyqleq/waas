The company kind of morphed into a site that SEO consultants and companies now use to gain SEO knowledge on a wide basis. There is a forum where questions can be answered for SEOmoz pro members. SEOmoz also offers a variety of SEO tools companies can use to improve their SEO.<\/p>\n<p>And of course SEOmoz still maintains a successful blog including a member blog <a href=\"http:\/\/www.seomoz.org\/ugc\">Youmoz<\/a>.<\/p>\n<p>Rand&#8217;s company is proof that blogging can not only improve your business it may be essential to success. SEOmoz&#8217;s blog generates scores of new visits and has thousands of subscribers. All of those people are either already pro members or potential pro members. The company&#8217;s model was built on writing content people wanted.<\/p>\n<p>In the post about why you should blog, Rand points out some simple understandings about business &#8211; people will buy from you because they know you, like you, and trust you.<\/p>\n<p>Your business blog can accomplish all of these things over time as you share your insight, personality, and reflections with your potential customers.<\/p>\n<p><em><a href=\"http:\/\/www.flickr.com\/photos\/notsogoodphotography\/2017430623\/\">Falling<\/a> image courtesy of <a href=\"http:\/\/www.flickr.com\/photos\/notsogoodphotography\/2017430623\/\">notsogoodphotography<\/a><\/em><\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"<p>Today I came across a blog post on one of my favorites sites &#8211; SEOmoz.org.
